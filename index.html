What is MapHook?

Out and about?

Use MapHook to search for interesting places near you. View other people’s Hooks that they have shared or look at what’s trending near you from Yahoo, Twitter and Foursquare. See what’s covered in Wikipedia near you or checkout Yelp reviews of nearby restaurants, shops, etc.

MapHook displays it all! Simply look at the MapHook map and watch the pins magically appear. Or do your own search. It’s that easy.

Your Digital Memory

Hooks are like digital memories.

Whether it’s an individual, a group or the rest of the world, you decide who you want to share your stuff with, create a bunch of groups! You can even send a Hook by email to a non MapHook member or push your Hooks to Twitter and Facebook. When you see your friend’s latest Hook – you can “like it” and leave them a comment! Be social, have fun and create beautiful Hooks!

Share Your Hooks!

Got Friends? Share Hooks!

Whether it’s an individual, a group or the public at large, you decide who you want to share your stuff with, create a bunch of groups! You can even send a hook by email to a non MapHook member.

You can also create stories by combining several hooks with pictures and share them with your friends and family. Add a comment or link to a Hook that has been shared with you on the web or using your mobile phone.
